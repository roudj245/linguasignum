What is LINGUA SIGNUM ?

Lingua Signum is a cultural event where participants discover different languages, learn their historical backgrounds, and connect with cultures and people from around the world through language.....

Discover Languages

Embark on a journey through the world's sounds. Every language opens a window to a new way of thinking, connecting, and living.

Learn History

Dive into the roots that shaped civilizations. Understand the present through the powerful stories carried within each word.

Connect

Turn language into a bridge for hearts and minds. True connection begins when words transform into friendship and understanding.
